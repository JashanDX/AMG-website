Who Am I?

Hey! I'm Jashandeep Singh with a deep fascination for the cosmos and a love for trekking mountain trails under open skies. I bridge the world of engineering with the quiet wonder of stargazing.

By day I write codes and solve problems. By night I shoot starry photos, share my observations and chase dark sky spots on mountain ridges where light pollution can't reach.

This site is my corner of the internet — tech-projects, ideas and the occasional photo from 1,466 feet above sea level. Moreover a portfolio of my work and experiences.
